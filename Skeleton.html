ANATOMY

This is a drawing that I made in my figure drwaing class. I created this drawing fully out of charcoal. I chose to do a face of a human to challenge myself, as I tend to avoid drawing human faces and I liked the challenge. I also was tasked to draw parts of a skeleton so I chose to do a side by side of a human face and a skull to see the difference in anatomy. I am so happy with the outcome of this project and it is one of my favorite renderings of a human I have completed.
