BLACK BUTTERFLIES & DEJA VU

Black Butterflies & Deja Vu is a printmaking project I made with lithography. I have a huge interest in figure drawing and drawing the human body, particularly women. In this project I really wanted to create something that was whimsical, feminine, and beautiful. My prompt was life and death, so I chose a woman to symbolize life and then added black butterflies that represented death. I made six copies of this print and I truly enjoyed the process of lithography, I love that it feels like traditional drawing. Printing this was an amazing and long process, but I would definitely do it again.
